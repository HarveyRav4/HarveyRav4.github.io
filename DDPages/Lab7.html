Thanksgiving Holiday Project

Happy Thanksgiving!

Thanksgiving is held on the fourth Thursday in November every year!

Thanksgiving is all about spending time with your family and friends and showing them how much you love and appreciate them!

What do people do for Thanksgiving?

  • People cook large meals for family and friends
  • People also host dinner parties

Some people serve food to the homeless on Thanksgiving!

What do people make for Thanksgiving?

  • Ham
  • Mac and Cheese
  • Chicken
  • Mashed Potatoes
